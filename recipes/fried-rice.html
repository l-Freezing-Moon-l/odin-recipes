Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a great dish to use leftover rice for. It's easily customizable and tastes great. Not to mention filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups leftover rice
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs, scrambled
  • Frozen veggies
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Steam frozen veggies until tender
  2. Add butter and rice, mix occasionally
  3. Cook eggs and add to rice
  4. Season with soy, salt, and pepper to taste
