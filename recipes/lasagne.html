Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish. Likely in the top three someone would think of when asked to name an Italian dish. Especially if you ask Garfield.

This dish is really easy to make and consists of only a handful of ingredients. One pan of lasagne is enough to feed the whole family for a few days. Especially when served with garlic bread and a salad. Unless you have an orange cat that is. Then you will only get the bread and green stuff.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Bolognese
  • Mozzarella and Parmesan
  • Ricotta
  • Salty Water

Steps

  1. Boil and shock the pasta sheets
  2. Add a layer of bolo to a lasagne dish and top with a sheet of pasta
  3. Add another layer of bolo
  4. Add some ricotta, mozz, and parm. Top with another pasta sheet
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for 5 layers
  6. This time put a thick layer of mozz and parm on top instead of the pasta sheet
  7. Bake until done
