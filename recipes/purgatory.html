Eggs in Purgatory

Description

A delicious rustic Italian dish consisting of spicy red sauce, vegetables, and eggs.

This classic should be eaten with toasted bread. It is a quick and easy meal that is delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • Spicy tomato sauce
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Ciabatta
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Dump spicy red sauce in a pan
  2. Add peppers, onions, and mushrooms
  3. Crack eggs into sauce
  4. Bake along with the bread until eggs are over easy
